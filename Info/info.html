展覽概念

將我們的創作靈魂匯集成獸的存在，

進駐媒體，

留下痕跡而形成作品。

觀者透過探索更多創作者的局部，

反射自身，

與我們共同映現獸的全貌。

By integrating our creative souls

into the presence of 獸 (Shou),

we enter the media and

leave traces to form the work.

Viewers reflect himself or herself in the works

by exploring parts of creators.

Together with us,

we reflect the whole picture of the 獸 (Shou).
